Allergies: Peanuts, peas (and all other legumes such as lentils), nuts (anything processed with peanuts or nuts), potatoes (french fries, sweet potatoes, anything fried in the same oil as french fries), mustard (in most wasabi), beans, soy (edamame, miso, tofu), shellfish (shrimp, crab, lobster, clams, oysters), cucumbers (and pickles), zucchini, squash, cumin (in all curry)
Food that is OK: Almonds, soy sauce, soybean oil, soy lecithin
